In life we have many goals. We want to have friends, relationships, a good job, but one of the most important considerations is what makes us happy. In the next few slides, we will try to find the truth about what really makes us happy. Is it our career? Money? Relationships? Let's explore!

In order to examine this question, I used data from https://github.com/megagonlabs/HappyDB . These researchers had people submit "Happy moments" that they experienced either in the past 24 hours, or within the last 3 months. They also collected demographic data for each participant. Here is the citation for their paper if you are interested.

Akari Asai, Sara Evensen, Behzad Golshan, Alon Halevy, Vivian Li, Andrei Lopatenko, Daniela Stepanov, Yoshihiko Suhara, Wang-Chiew Tan, Yinzhan Xu, ``HappyDB: A Corpus of 100,000 Crowdsourced Happy Moments'', LREC '18, May 2018. (to appear)
